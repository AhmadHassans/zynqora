❓ FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take? ▼
Depends on scope. A landing page takes 1–2 weeks. A full mobile app (iOS + Android) typically takes 6–10 weeks. We give a detailed timeline during the discovery call so you always know what to expect.
Do you publish apps to App Store and Google Play? ▼
Yes. We handle the full submission process for both App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android) — including screenshots, descriptions, and review navigation. You just need a developer account.
What is your payment structure? ▼
We typically work on a 50% upfront / 50% on delivery basis for fixed-scope projects. For larger projects, we can split into milestone-based payments. We accept bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa, and PayPal.
Can you work with our existing codebase or design? ▼
Absolutely. We regularly take over existing projects, fix bugs, add features, or redesign UI. We'll do a brief code audit first so we can give you an accurate estimate.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance after launch? ▼
Yes — all plans include a post-launch support window (2 weeks to 3 months depending on the plan). After that, we offer flexible monthly maintenance retainers. We don't disappear after shipping.
Who owns the code and IP after delivery? ▼
You do. Full source code, design files, and all assets are transferred to you upon final payment. For enterprise clients, we also provide a signed NDA and IP ownership agreement upfront.
